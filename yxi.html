passion.

passion in many different forms. passion for this world, our planet that is burning to ash as we speak.

Passion in human connection: conversation, friendship, romance. Emotional and physical.

Passion in peace, freedom and respect for one another, laying down weapons and engaging in conversation. Standing up and fighting for what is right.

Passion for old stories and ancient legends, what we can learn from them even in this day and age.

Passion for spirituality, the longing for a higher power, divine experiences, an explanation to countless occurences in our world.

And ultimately passion for beauty and art, how art elevates life and makes it beautiful. How art deals with different topics of our lives and sparks discussion. How art helps us see beauty in our lives. How art is not luxury or elitist. How art should be accessible to everyone.

Enter the world of Kaikkivaltias. A vibrant, lush finnish summer set in a romantic time long gone.
